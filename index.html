Business or Corporate Websites




Business or corporate websites are used by companies to provide information about their products or services, and often include pages like “About Us,” “Services,” and “Contact Us.” Costs can vary based on the size and complexity of the site.

E-commerce Websites




E-commerce websites are designed to sell products or services online. They require more complex features, like “shopping carts”,“ product pages”, and secure “payment gateways”. The cost of an e-commerce website can vary widely, depending if it`s a small website. or a platform like Shopify or WooCommerce, custom-built online store.

Personal or Portfolio Websites



Personal or portfolio websites are typically used by individuals to showcase their work, whether that’s art, writing, design, or other projects. These websites tend to be relatively good, usually consisting of a few pages like a home page, about page, and a page to display the portfolio. For a basic personal website, you might only need to budget for a domain name, hosting, and a premium theme.

News or magazine websites are content-heavy sites that require a robust CMS to manage numerous articles, authors, and categories. They also need to handle high traffic volumes. Costs can vary widely, “simple blog-style news site”, “custom-designed news” or “magazine website” cost is large.
